Welcome to my website. I am an ornithologist and evolutionary biologist. My research program examines mechanisms responsible for broad patterns of organismal diversity using comparative studies at the population level. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the lab of <a href="https://derryberrylab.wordpress.com/people/" target="_blank">Liz Derryberry</a> at the University of Tennessee - Knoxville.
